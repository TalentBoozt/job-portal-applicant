What is Talent Boozt ?

Talent Boozt is a simple, yet powerful recruitment platform. Employers can post their vacant jobs with just a few clicks and can track and monitor applicants easily. Candidates can easily apply for jobs by creating an account and completing their profile.

Do you offer any other services ?

Currently, our main focus is on the recruitment platform, we are expertise in all areas of HR and HR information systems. We have exiting HR systems in our production pipeline which companies can soon take advantage of.

Is Talent Boozt free to use ?

Talent Boozt is free for all candidates. However, for companies, while the basic package is free of charge, the pro pacakge and the premium package include monthly subscription fee.

Is Talent Boozt safer to use with my account ?

We are guaranteed your data will not send or shared with third parties and we only use your data to enhance user experiences and make your platform a better place.
